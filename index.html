We recognize that mental health and addiction challenges can impact anyone, regardless of background or circumstance. Our organization is committed to providing compassionate, recovery oriented care and holistic support to individuals, families, and communities.

To work together to create a supportive environment that foster recovery, resilience and a better quality of life for individuals with mental health challenges through family involvement, after care planning, psych education, individualized treatment plan and therapeutic intervention.

Our comprehensive approach integrates personalized treatment, skill development, social integration and evidence based practices, empowering individuals to overcome adversity, achieve stability, and thrive. We strive to create a supportive environment that fosters resilience, hope and a sense of belonging, ultimately facilitating successful rehabilitation and reintegration into society.

Staff Code Of Conduct

1. PROFESSIONALISM

Maintain a high level of professionalism in all interactions Dress appropriately and adhere to the facilities dress code Arrive on time and be prepared for all shifts and clients sessions.

2. CONFIDENTIALITY

Respect the confidentiality of clients in SHAPE REHAB. Do not disclose any personal information without a proper authorization. Secure all records and information related to clients.

3. INTEGRITY

Be honest and transparent in all communications. Avoid conflicts of interest and disclose any potential conflicts to supervisors. Adhere to all ethical guidelines set forth by the management of SHAPE REHAB.

4. RESPECT

Treat all clients, families and colleague with dignity and respect. Promote an inclusive environment free from discrimination and harassment.

5. RESPONSIBILITY

Provide high quality care and support to clients. Follow all policies and procedures related to client treatment and safety. Seek supervision or consultation when face with challenging situation.
